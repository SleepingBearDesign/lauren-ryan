Parking at Luna

Metered parking is available on the streets surrounding Luna, but can be difficult to find on a Friday evening. These meters range from $1.25 per hour to $1.75 per hour and are only enforced until 6:00 pm, unless they are marked otherwise. If interested in the metered parking, consider downloading the Parkmobile app - it allows you to pay for your meter with a credit card and makes metered parking stress free!
